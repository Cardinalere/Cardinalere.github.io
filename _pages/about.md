Hi! I am an M.S. student in Artificial Intelligence at Renmin University of China, advised by Prof. Yankai Lin. Before that, I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science and Technology from Shandong University. I am broadly interested in large language models (LLMs) and natural language processing (NLP), especially reasoning in LLMs.
